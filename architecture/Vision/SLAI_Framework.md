# Stellar Logic AI Framework (SLAI Framework)

Version: 1.0

Author: Jamie Brown

Created: June 2026

---

# Purpose

The SLAI Framework is the foundational architecture used to build all Stellar Logic AI operating systems.

Rather than building products independently, SLAI develops reusable systems that can be applied across multiple industries.

Every SLAI product is built from a common set of operational, analytical, security, and automation components.

---

# AI Development Philosophy

SLAI will initially leverage existing AI platforms
to accelerate development and customer value.

Over time, SLAI will build proprietary intelligence
using data generated from its operating systems.

The long-term objective is not to compete with
general-purpose AI providers.

The objective is to build specialized intelligence
that understands the industries and operational
domains served by SLAI products.
